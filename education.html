Education to ensure conservation

KCR will involve the community in the stewardship of coral reefs. Locally-based collaboration and education efforts improves ecosystem management while enhancing long term sustainability. KCR seeks to engage the following audiences in its work:

PROGRAMS COMING SOON!

1

General Public

Educate locals and visitors about the local reef, ocean health and kuleana through strategic outreach activities such as publications and social media.

2

K - 12

Outreach to students through presentations and coral reef activity kits.

3

Graduate and Undergraduate

Train college students on cutting-edge monitoring and data analysis of coral reef ecosystem health. Grad students will also be given opportunities to design and execute novel reef restoration research on Hawaiian coral reefs. Interested?
